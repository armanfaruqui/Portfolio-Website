Esthetica is a tool, platform, and format used to create and share aesthetic experiences. Each 'esthetic' conists of a combination of images, videos, and texts, sandwiched by a pantone pallete at the top, and a embedded playlist at the bottom. Once created, the content can be visualized in a variety of ways,and easily shared on social media. The app is currently in the early stages of development.
