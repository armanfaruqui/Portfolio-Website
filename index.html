Altermode

Community-based clothing repair and creative reuse. Alter-mode brings together asylum-seeking women from Parc-Extension that have years of sewing, tailoring, and textile experience.

Through regular repair and alteration events, community members are invited to bring clothes they love that need some TLC. These pieces upcycled to help Montrealers waste less and share more.

By extending the life of clothing and centering skill-sharing, and social connection, Altermode contributes to sustainable fashion practices while fostering integration, economic participation, and stronger community ties within Parc-Extension and beyond.

Over 300 items of clothing upcycled
